Detailed Description

Represents a trash can with Contacts deleted less than RETENTION_PERIOD_DAYS days from a ContactList.

The trash can is implemented as a map of LocalDateProperty keys and SetProperty values. The map is ordered by the date of deletion of the contacts.

Invariant
trashCan != null
